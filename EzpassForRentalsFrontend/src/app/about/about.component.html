Use of this program.

This program is here to help for car rental companies who
struggle with the problem that people who rent cars drive
through toll booths and it automatically charges the cars ezpass.

From now on, just enter every customer's information when they
take the car, and when you get the ezpass statement just upload it
to the program, and the program will charge all the customers responsible
for the charges.!

*(As this is a demo, its not charging the cards just returning the list to be charged)
